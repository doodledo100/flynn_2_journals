What are the threats to wifi security listed in the article?
The real threat is a MiTM hacker between you and a free access point, relaying all of your data to the AP after it goes through the hackers computer.
What can you do to mitigate those threats?
Use a VPN, turn WiFi off when not in use, keep and updated virus/malware scanner on your computer.
What is a vpn? How does one use a VPN? Why?
A VPN is a virtual private network that encrypt all of your traffic before it sent to the AP. This should be used to keep you safe from MiTM threats.
