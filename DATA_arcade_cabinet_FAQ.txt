DATA Arcade Machine Upright Cabinet FAQ:

What will I be doing in this project?
I will design and build a modern arcade upright cabinet for use in testing and playing DATA students games created and developed in DATA programming courses.

How long will this project take roughly?
Approx. 2 to 2.5 months (55 - 60 school days)

Why was this project considered?
I think it will be a fun and cool project to complete. I also have also desired building an arcade cabinet to play some of the classic games that have gone away.

How will the cabinet be used by DATA?
DATA may use the arcade cabinet for recruiting events to showcase to future DATA students the video games created by current DATA programmers.
